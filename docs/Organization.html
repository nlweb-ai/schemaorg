Organization

An organization such as a school, NGO, corporation, club, etc.

Subclass of: Thing
More specific types: Airline, Cooperative, Corporation, GovernmentOrganization, LocalBusiness, MedicalOrganization, NGO, PerformingGroup, PoliticalParty, SportsOrganization, WorkersUnion
